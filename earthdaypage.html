What are the issues revolving clothing waste?:

Clothing waste is the quick discarding of clothing material primarily to purchase more. This phenomenon is also known as "Fast Fashion" and can major negative impacts such as Greenhouse-Gas emissions, water consumption, overconsumption and negative social consequences.
