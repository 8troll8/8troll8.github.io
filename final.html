Final project

In this game, you play as a hero that has the main objective of rescuing the non corrupt bitmojis that are being held captive by the bitlord. Upon clicking start, you will be teleported directly into the game where your very first enemy will be charging at you. In order to defend yourself, you must use the arrow keys to move around and the "alt" button to pull out your sword. Afterwards, the next level will be a bit more difficult due to the extra mobs that will be introduced.
